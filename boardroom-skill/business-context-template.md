## Usage Notes

- Be specific and honest - your advisors need truth, not aspirations
- Update this document as your business evolves (quarterly recommended)
- Include anything that might affect strategic decisions
- Don't worry about confidentiality - this stays local to your machine
- If uncertain about numbers, provide your best estimate with "[estimated]" note
